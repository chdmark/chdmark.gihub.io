Growing Pains of Developing a Website

Developing a personal website was fun and rewarding. Starting from scratch and learning the ins and out of CSS and HTML was a learning process, but I’m glad I learned the basic foundations. At first, CSS was incredibly frustrating because of how tedious it was to position elements. In addition, implementing a responsive webpage is difficult as well. I think the most challenging part was implementing your wireframe to look modern and unique. CSS has many tools at your disposal, but you can’t apply advanced concepts without learning the basics. I wanted to do many things with my website, but found it time consuming because of the other tasks I had to complete by the end of the week. transformational experience.
